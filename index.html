Smoking Awareness

Raising awareness about the dangers of smoking and promoting a healthier lifestyle.

Health Risks of Smoking

Lung Cancer

Smoking is the leading cause of lung cancer, responsible for thousands of deaths each year.

Heart Disease

Smoking increases the risk of heart disease and high blood pressure, leading to severe health issues.

Ways to Quit Smoking

1. Seek professional help and counseling.

2. Use nicotine replacement therapy.

3. Stay active and maintain a healthy lifestyle.
